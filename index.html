Victonale Academy is a premier co-educational institution offering world-class education from Pre-Primary through to Secondary level. Founded in 1987, we have grown into one of East Africa's most respected schools, producing outstanding alumni who lead in every field — from medicine and engineering to arts, law, and public service.

Our holistic approach to education ensures that every student receives not only academic excellence but also develops strong character, leadership skills, emotional intelligence, and a spirit of service to the community.
